Goa is the smallest state in India but also the most laid back and the most popular with tourists, both from India and all over the world. Most tourists flock here in the Winter months to enjoy sunny days, blissful beaches, fresh seafood, spicy curries, vibrant markets, wild nightlife and much more, all infused with the special and captivating blend of Portuguese and Indian cultures. There really is something for everyone in Goa, from luxury hotels and world-class fine dining restaurants to mellow yoga retreats, to adventure sports to wild all-night beach parties, to cheap and cheerful beach huts and hippie markets.
